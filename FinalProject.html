My Mission

If you are a someone seeking adventure, or curious about the Incan Empire and its remains, or a local proud of your roots I invite you to experience Cusco, the heart of the Andes. By sharing my experiences with traveling, tour guides, and local festivities, I can give you insight on what to expect and be prepared for when visiting Cusco. Cusco a place with many ruins to visit that teach us about Inca culture, there are many tour guides that can take you around the sacred valley, meauseums. I will show you things to look out for and show you what you can expect based on what dates you decide to go and visit my beautiful home country.
