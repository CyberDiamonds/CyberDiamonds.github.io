Lima Capitol

The capital of Peru, as of now it is the only airport to get to Cusco. An airport is in in the works to have direct flights to cusco so that is exciting. Here you can take a couple of days and enjoy plenty of the beutiful side of Lima which is Miraflores mostly. Here you can enjoy the heart of food if you want to try good cuisine here would be the place as there are many restaurants and highly trained chefs here. The downside is that it is quite dangerous for tourists and even for locals. So be very aware of your surroundings and don't get lost and fall into tourist traps. And be prepared for the long traffic it is probably worse than new york!

Cusco City

The home land of the Incas here is where you can explore a lot of the cultural side of Peru the history of Incas and Spain. There are churches meuseums, archeological parks. And also a lot of artesinal stores you can visit and take a part of cusco with you. Be it little llama dolls, hand made jewelery, stones and other hand made items.

Macchupicchu

Here you are near to the amazon rain forest so expect it to be much more humid and hot here, the nights will remain chilly however. Bring your bug spray and comfortable shoes as you will walk alot in this city. They have banned the use of cars in the city and tehre will be a lot of bikes and carriges. Busses are the only allowed that transport people to the mountain Machupichhu it is really an unforgettable sight. There is history and now has changes on how you can get in and explore as regulation is being placed to avoid the sinking of Machupichhu
