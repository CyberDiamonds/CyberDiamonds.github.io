Who are we?

Just a small team of two people, both born in Cusco!

Jewel

The website developer for this site! She was born in Cusco but raised in New Jersey and New York for most of her life. Often visiting her homecountry and learning something new about it each time. With her knowledge in coding she wanted to share her discoveries and wanted to give guides to fellow Americans who would love to visit someday and also explore areas of Cusco that are beyond tourist sites and taking you through my experience as a Peruvian American whos really proud of her home country.

"I hope you enjoyed viewing what Peru and Cusco have to offer and are now prepared to make the jouurney your own!""

Hector

The cusco expert tour guide and photographer. He had extensive study on becoming a tour guide in Cusco for 4 years.
