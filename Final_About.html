Who are we?

Just a small team of two people, both born in Cusco!

Jewel

The website developer for this site! She was born in Cusco but raised in New Jersey and New York for most of her life. Often visiting her homecountry and learning something new about it each time. With her knowledge in coding she wanted to share her discoveries and wanted to give guides to fellow Americans who would love to visit someday and also explore areas of Cusco that are beyond tourist sites and taking you through my experience as a Peruvian American whos really proud of her home country.

"I hope you enjoyed viewing what Peru and Cusco have to offer and are now prepared to make the jouurney your own!""

Hector

The cusco expert tour guide and photographer. He had extensive study on becoming a tour guide in Cusco for several years. And endured many From extreme hikes or river rafting he also has studid the history of the churches and meuseams and has gone to various spots in Cusco. He was born and raised there and wanted to contribute his knowledge to this website and guide fellow tourists to stay safe and informed prior to arrival. He can assist in your tours in English and Italian!

"I hope you enjoyed viewing what Peru and Cusco have to offer and are now prepared to make the jouurney your own!""

Katana

The teams social media lead! She was raised in Cusco and loves sharing images and attending events that occur. However she likes to remain behind the scenes and lets her eyes be for you to see what she sees. Her wish is to keep showing the world what Peru is about and that Cusco should remain protected and maintained. She loves teaching the ways of the people their language quirks, the rules, and areas to be and tourists should be taght especially if they don't have relatives there.
