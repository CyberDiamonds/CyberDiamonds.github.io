Preface

Why record and share about my trash? Well I have found out that we can always learn more about ourselves if we just take a moment to think about it. Some track their meals others activity level, well why not trash? It is something that we do almost without thinking too much about besides is this done with its value? Then we trash it or give it a different purpose but there are things we can learn about ourselves in the process of taking a moment and ask yourself what does throwing this away say about me.

Profile(Item of the Week)

The item is an old wallet I have had for many years I would say about plus 7 years approximately. It's dark brown and leathery uite hefty and big for a now empty wallet. I acquired it when I was deep cleaning a storage space of mine and found this wallet along with a black smaller one. This one is going to the trash because the black one I could reuse and this one just is too big. Also the leather is peeling off on the inside so too old aswell.

What i found out about the wallet is that it is a Buxton, and has a velvet tough cowhide exterior. They are still a running buissness and advocate for responsible resourcing according to their website.

As for my specific wallet it was hard to find the exact one I had must be an very old model and has an accessory clip that you can add more pockets into your wallet for cards or spare house keys even. The Buxton company originated from Dana Buxton and Julia Buxton.
